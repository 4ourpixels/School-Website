Intermediate

Advanced design techniques, such as typography, layout design, digital design, and possibly internships or practicum experiences in the design industry. These services aim to provide students with more specialized skills, and prepare them for professional level design positions.

Degree

A Graphic Design degree program covers a wide range of design principles, techniques, software, foundation courses and specializations, preparing students for a career in the field.

Post Graduation

Post-graduates will have the knowledge and skills to work as professional designers in different industries. They will have a portfolio of their work, and may have gained real-world experience through internships. Also, they can choose to work as freelancers or starting their own design business.
